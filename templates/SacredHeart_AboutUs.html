Our Vision, Mission, and Goals

VISION:

Sacred Heart Medical Center envisions to be a teaching, training, and research hospital and the pioneer center for cancer prevention, detection, and management, north of Metro Manila, by 2015.

MISSION:

The mission of SHMC is to provide state-of-the-art facilities that aid our duly-accredited medical staff in the accurate diagnosis and management of multi-disciplinary disorders and to allow our competent hospital staff to render equal opportunities for all patients to enjoy the quality of care that they deserve, at affordable cost.

GOALS:

    To be an advocate of holistic care, focusing not only on the physical or physiologic needs of its patients but also on their emotional and spiritual needs.

    To employ well-trained, duly-accredited and highly respected doctors to provide their intellectual expertise on the medical and surgical care of their patients.

    To employ duly-licensed, competent, compassionate and efficient medical, nursing, and non-medical staff to guarantee team effort in the delivery of quality health care, in response to the needs of patients.

    To encourage, motivate and support continuing educational programs for professional enhancement and personality improvement that will promote progressive growth and stability of the medical center.

Our Core Values

Leadership

The Board of Trustees of SHMC and the other key officials of the hospital, duly chosen for their leadership abilities and managerial expertise, are committed to spearhead the successful operations of the hospital, in collaboration with all their partners in hospital management, sharing a common vision and mission for SHMC.

Commitment to Excellence

The SHMC family will continuously strive for excellence in the performance of their respective duties and responsibilities as they work in unity and harmony for the welfare and benefit of the patients.

Patient Satisfaction as a Social Responsibility

SHMC shall uphold its social responsibility to guarantee patient satisfaction through efficient, effective, competent, compassionate, and affordable health care, respecting each and every patient’s unique medical or surgical needs.

Awards & Certifications

    Initially licensed by the Department of Health as 50-bed Level 3 Special Service Hospital (Cancer & Cardiovascular Centre) on September 22, 2009

    Upgraded by DOH as 75-bed General Hospital Level 3 on February 25, 2011

    Re-Classified by DOH as 75-bed General Hospital Level 2 since January 1, 2013

    Initially accredited by the Philippine Health Insurance Corporation (PhilHealth) as a 50-bed Level 3 Hospital on November 9, 2009

    Accredited by PhilHealth as “Center of Quality” since May 10, 2010

    Granted TUV Rheinland ISO 9001:2008 Certificate Provision in Intensive Care Unit valid from December 20, 2010 until April 28, 2013
